Guiding Children’s First Steps Since 1988

Tutor Time® was established in 1988 in Florida, USA. We have more than 500 corporate and affiliated schools throughout the United States, Europe, Hong Kong and Indonesia. Currently, Tutor Time® is one of the largest provider of preschool and educational services in the United States.

Tutor Time® in Hong Kong was established in 2000, with the first two schools opened at the same time in 2001 at Kowloon Tong and Red Hill Plaza. It was followed by Tutor Time® WOW at Kowloon Tong in 2005, Tutor Time® Braemar Hill in 2008, Tutor Time® Mid-Levels in 2010, and Tutor Time® WOW Central in 2012.

Since our schools' opening, all Tutor Time® schools have attained “Schools of Excellence" with scores of 95% or above on the annual evaluations from Tutor Time® USA.

For two years in a row (2012 - 2013), Tutor Time® has been selected to receive the “Readers' Choice Award” by the readers of “The List" magazine in Hong Kong.
